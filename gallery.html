Gallery of non-technical things

A selection of photographs taken with actual cameras, toned with old school Lightroom Classic and a bit of Darktable. No Photoshop, no AI, no Flux Kontext, no Nano-Banana. Photographs captured over the last decade with Nikon D60, Canon 60D, Mamiya C330, Minolta X-700, Sony A7C-II, Samsung Galaxy S8 and iPhone 13 Pro.

Licensing, attribution and AI use

Every image on this site is licensed under CC BY-NC-ND 4.0. Why you would want to do anything commercial with it is beyond me. But if you do, you'll need my permission by reaching out to me.

I'm taking cues from Creative Commons' CC Signals (preference signals for dataset reuse) and the EFF community's push for explicit Creative Commons licensing. In that spirit, this site sets noai/noimageai directives and these images are not granted for training, fine-tuning or evaluation of any AI system. I know this is futile. But ah well. Appearances and that.
